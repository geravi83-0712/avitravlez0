Can you book the flight?
Good rule of thumb: Anything above 1.5 cents per point is a solid redemption. Above 2 cents is great. Below 1 cent means you are probably better off paying cash and saving your miles for a premium cabin flight.
Always verify award availability with the airline before transferring points. Transfers are usually not reversible.
